# 2. Playbook: Strategy & Campaign Architecture

Design the complete campaign architecture based on the research from stage 1.

## What to produce

1. **Funnel diagnosis**: Why prospects aren't converting. Top 5-7 objections with estimated frequency. Where in the funnel most are lost.
2. **Campaign architecture** (2-4 campaigns covering the full funnel):
   - TOFU (awareness/education)
   - BOFU (retargeting/closing)
   - Social Proof (testimonials, case studies)
   - B2B-specific (if applicable)
   For each: name, description, target audience, objective, pain points, objections, success metrics.
3. **Content matrix per campaign**: Define categorization columns (postsColumns) with possible values:
   - Category (benefit, objection, social proof type)
   - Tone/angle
   - Product dimension (pain point, feature)
   - Variant (urgency, credibility, ROI)
4. **Platform distribution**: Instagram (~60%) + Facebook (~40%). Formats: feed images, carousels, reels.
5. **Production methods**: Figma Static (55-65%), ElevenLabs (15-25%), Replit Animation (10-15%), Video (0-5%).
6. **Funnel diagram**: How campaigns connect, retargeting audiences, cross-posting.

## Output

Save everything as `campana-arquitectura-2026.md` in the location the user specifies.

**DO NOT create campaigns or posts in Lexguard yet.** Only the documented strategy for approval.
