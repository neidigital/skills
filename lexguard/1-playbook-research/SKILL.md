# 1. Playbook: Research & Discovery

Research a product before designing marketing campaigns. This is stage 1 of 7 in the campaign creation playbook.

## When to use

Use this as the FIRST step when creating marketing campaigns for a new product. The user should provide:
- The product name
- Where to find product documentation
- Where to find existing marketing materials

## Steps

1. **Read product documentation**: Find and read the product briefing, branding guidelines, voice & tone, features, and differentiators.
2. **Read existing marketing materials**: Find pain-points docs, benefits docs, content matrix, communication guidelines, and existing posts.
3. **Find real data**: Search for real customer testimonials, success stories, impact metrics (savings, ROI), and market data.
4. **Competitive research**: Search the web for the product's main competitors in Mexico. Identify what content they publish on Instagram/Facebook. Note gaps to exploit.
5. **Ask strategic questions**: After reading everything, ask the user intelligent questions about:
   - Available ad budget
   - Primary objective (awareness, leads, conversions, retention)
   - Ideal existing customers (for testimonials)
   - Common sales objections
   - What has worked / not worked in previous marketing
   - Timeline and urgency
   - Available production resources (design, video, etc.)

**DO NOT propose any strategy yet.** Only research + questions. Understand the product deeply before designing.

## Next stage

After completing research and getting answers, proceed to **/2-playbook-strategy**.
